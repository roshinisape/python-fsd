Anirudh Ravichander

Anirudh Ravichander, mononymously known as Anirudh, is a prolific Indian music composer and playback singer who primarily works in the Tamil film industry, with significant contributions to Telugu and Hindi cinema.

Career & Achievements

  • Debut: He shot to global fame at age 21 with the viral hit "Why This Kolaveri Di" from the 2012 film 3.
  • Major Works: Notable soundtracks include Kaththi, Petta, Master, Vikram, Jailer, Leo, and his full-fledged Hindi debut Jawan.
  • Recent Hits: As of early 2026, he has released tracks like "Aaya Sher" and "Vaaraan Dei" from The Paradise, and "Aaja Raja" from the KH x RK Reunion project.
  • Concerts: He is currently on his Rockstar Anirudh XV Tour, celebrating 15 years in the industry, with a major show scheduled for March 21, 2026, in Hyderabad.
  • Business: In February 2026, he launched his own record label Albuquerque Records.

Personal Life

  • Family: He is the son of actor Ravi Raghavendra and the nephew of superstar Rajinikanth.
  • Rumours: Recent reports and social media speculation have linked him romantically to Kavya Maran (CEO of Sunrisers Hyderabad), though Anirudh has dismissed marriage rumours.
